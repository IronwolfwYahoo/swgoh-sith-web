Calculations Used
  • Food Needed To Break Even by Level
    • Definition: FspSR = Food Spent Per Soul Received
    • Calculation: FspSR = (Food per Run / (Souls Received Per Run x Multiplier))

    • Definition: TFN = Total Food Needed; TSN = Total Souls Needed
    • TSR x FspSR = TFN

  • Time Needed for Portal to Stay Open
    • Definition: IF = Initial Food; FPH = Food Per Hour
    • Calculation: (TFN - IF)/FPH = Hours Needed
